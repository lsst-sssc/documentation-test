Solar system objects in DP0.2¶

LSST’s Data Preview v0.2 is simulation of 300 deg2 of sky, observed with an LSST-like camera and cadence. Solar system objects are not included in the simulated DP0.2 data, but one can still search the DP0.2 metadata for coverage of a their ephemerides. This document serves as a resource to share information on those targets.

sbsearch results (lead: Mike Kelley)¶

sbsearch is a library that is designed to search for comets and asteroids in wide-field time-domain data sets. v3 of sbsearch is in development (as of Jan 2025), but has been successfully used to find solar system objects in DP0.2 data.
